## Notes
- **Personalize the opener.** One specific line ("I've shopped [Company]'s [location] for years" / "saw [Association] is running [event]") beats any polished template.
- **For LinkedIn DM/InMail:** trim to the first two sentences + the link. Drop the subject line.
- **Warm contacts:** cut the "I run RuggedAI" intro and lead with the ask — they already know you.
