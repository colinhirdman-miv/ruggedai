# Pricing Guide (Rough Ranges for Call Qualification)

Use these ranges to set honest expectations on calls. Never quote exact prices — always say "this is a general range and a tech will confirm after assessment."

## Service Call
- Diagnostic/trip charge: $95–$150 (applied to repair if we do the work)

## Common Residential Jobs
- Outlet replacement: $150–$250
- Switch replacement: $100–$200
- GFCI outlet installation: $150–$300
- Ceiling fan installation (no existing wiring): $250–$450
- Panel inspection: $150–$300
- Breaker replacement: $150–$300
- 200-amp panel upgrade: $2,500–$4,500
- EV charger installation (Level 2): $800–$1,800
- Whole-home surge protector: $300–$600

## Common Commercial Jobs
- Service call: $150–$250
- Dedicated circuit installation: $500–$1,500
- LED retrofit (per fixture): $100–$300
- Emergency lighting installation: $300–$600 per unit
- Panel upgrade (commercial): $5,000–$15,000+ depending on service size

## After-Hours Premium
- Emergency after-hours calls carry a $75–$150 after-hours surcharge on top of standard rates

## What Affects Pricing
- Age and accessibility of existing wiring
- Permit requirements (we always pull permits — this adds cost but protects you)
- Material costs (fluctuate with market)
- Scope of work discovered during assessment

## Key Message for Callers
"I can give you a general ballpark, but the best way to get an accurate number is to have one of our licensed technicians take a look. There's no obligation after the assessment."
