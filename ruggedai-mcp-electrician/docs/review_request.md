# Review Request Templates

## Text Message (send same day as job completion)

Hi [Customer Name], it's [Tech Name] from [Company Name]. Really glad we could get that taken care of for you today. If you have 60 seconds, we'd really appreciate a Google review — it helps us a lot. Here's the link: [Google Review Link]. Thanks again!

---

## Email (send within 24 hours of job completion)

Subject: How did we do? — [Company Name]

Hi [Customer Name],

Thanks for choosing [Company Name] for your [job description] today. We hope everything looks and works exactly as expected.

If you have a moment, we'd love to hear about your experience. Reviews help our small team reach more homeowners and businesses in [City/Area] who need reliable electrical work.

Leave us a Google review here: [Google Review Link]

It takes about 60 seconds and means a lot to us.

If anything wasn't right or you have any questions about the work we completed, please reach out directly at [Phone Number]. We stand behind everything we do.

Thanks again,
[Tech/Owner Name]
[Company Name]
[Phone Number]

---

## Follow-Up if No Review After 3 Days

Hi [Customer Name], just wanted to check in — hoping everything with your [job description] is working great. If you get a chance, that Google review link is still here: [Google Review Link]. No pressure at all, just wanted to make sure you saw it. Thanks!
