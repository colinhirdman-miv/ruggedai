# Services Offered

## Residential Services
- Panel upgrades and replacements
- Whole-home rewiring
- Outlet and switch installation/repair
- Ceiling fan installation
- EV charger installation (Level 1 and Level 2)
- Smoke and carbon monoxide detector installation
- GFCI outlet installation (kitchens, bathrooms, outdoors)
- Surge protection
- Lighting installation (interior and exterior)
- Generator hookup and transfer switches

## Commercial Services
- Panel upgrades and service upgrades
- Commercial wiring and rewiring
- LED lighting retrofits and upgrades
- Parking lot and exterior lighting
- Emergency lighting and exit signs
- Dedicated circuits for equipment
- Tenant build-outs
- Preventive maintenance programs
- Code compliance inspections and corrections

## Emergency Services (24/7)
- Power outages
- Sparking or smoking outlets/panels
- Tripped breakers that won't reset
- Burning smell from electrical sources
- Storm damage assessment and repair
- Flood-related electrical assessment

## What We Don't Do
- Appliance repair
- Low-voltage data/cable wiring (we refer out)
- Solar panel installation (we do the electrical hookup only)
